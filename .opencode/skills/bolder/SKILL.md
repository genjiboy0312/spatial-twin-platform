# Bolder

Use this flow when the design feels timid, bland, or visually interchangeable with every other template.

## Preparation

1. Load `impeccable` first.
2. If design context is missing, run `impeccable teach`.
3. Review `../impeccable/reference/typography.md`, `../impeccable/reference/color-and-contrast.md`, and `../impeccable/reference/spatial-design.md`.
4. Confirm how much visual risk the brand and feature can actually support.

## What to amplify

- hierarchy through larger scale jumps and clearer weight contrast,
- color through more decisive palette use instead of timid accents,
- composition through stronger focal points, asymmetry, or rhythm,
- and motion only where it reinforces the chosen direction.

## Rules

- Pick one personality direction and commit to it.
- Make one or two moves much stronger instead of making everything louder.
- Avoid purple-blue AI defaults, gradient text, glassy filler, nested cards, or generic "more effects" thinking.
- Keep body copy readable and actions obvious.

## Finish line

The result should feel more memorable and confident, not noisier or less usable.
