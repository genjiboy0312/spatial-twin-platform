# Arrange

This deprecated upstream wrapper is kept only so the local `impeccable` family stays complete in the local bundle.

The canonical upstream equivalent was `layout`, but this local bundle does not vendor `layout/` as a separate skill.

Use:
- `shape` when the goal is pre-code layout or information-flow planning,
- `impeccable` when the goal is integrated design direction,
- or `polish` when the layout mostly works and needs refinement.

Keep `arrange` present for completeness only. Do not treat it as a primary route.
